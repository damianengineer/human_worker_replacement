AI's effect on entry-level jobs isn't just theoretical anymore — it's
already showing up in hiring data, though it's concentrated in a handful
of jobs and industries, not spread evenly across the whole economy. Below,
you'll see where that stands right now, which jobs — including specific
programs at Stark State — are most exposed, what's currently protecting
other jobs from the same fate, and why some respected researchers think
the scarier predictions might not hold up.
